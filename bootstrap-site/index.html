About Our Store

Discover the right guitar for you at our store. We are home to a large collection of electric, acoustic and bass guitars, amplifiers, keyboards, microphones and more. We have 10 stores across the Europe, and we offer the fastest and easiest way to find all you need inone place. If you are worried about shipping cost, we offer free shipping on many items from ourcollection no matter where you are from. Here you are sure to find the best gear at the lowest price and due to our wonderful and skillful staff, you are guaranteed to have a nice and enjoyable experience while shopping at our centers.
